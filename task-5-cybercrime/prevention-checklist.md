Prevention Checklist

General Safety Tips:
- Use strong, unique passwords for each account
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA).
- Do not click on suspicious links in emails, SMS, or social media messages.
- Keep your devices and apps updated regularly.
- Avoid sharing personal information like address, phone number, or college ID online.
- Download apps only from trusted sources like official app stores.
- Be cautious when using public Wi-Fi
- avoid accessing sensitive accounts.

Financial / UPI Safety Tips:
- Never share OTP, PIN, or UPI credentials with anyone.
- Always verify UPI IDs and QR codes before making payments.
- Do not approve unknown “collect request” transactions.
- Enable transaction alerts and set spending limits.

Reporting Cybercrime:
- National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
- Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930
