 Social Media Checklist

Do’s
- Think before you post – consider how it may affect your reputation
-Use privacy settings to control who can see your content
-Verify information before sharing it
-Maintain a positive and professional tone in posts and comments
-Respect others online – be polite and avoid arguments

 Don’ts
- Don’t share personal or sensitive information (address, phone number, etc.)
- Don’t post inappropriate or offensive content
- Don’t engage in cyberbullying or trolling
- Don’t overshare every detail of your life
- Don’t ignore your digital footprint – what you post can stay online permanently
